Garden of Eden

High levels of trust and ample funding support smooth transition from classical methods to NAMs

Emma has an engineering background, while Adrian is a translational researcher. With their starting grant, they are developing NAMs for neurodegenerative diseases. Their collaboration, combining diverse expertise, aligns with efforts to refine animal experiments — ultimately leading to high-quality results and driving innovation.

Doomsday

Low trust and lack of resources lead to the collapse of biomedical research ecosystem in Europe

Alex is still striving to keep the dream of scientific excellence in biomedical research alive. However, it’s not easy—one of his top students recently left for a better offer in the private sector, and another is struggling with burnout.

Sophie’s choice

Sophie has recently completed her PhD and has a brilliant idea for an AI tool to enhance the Home Cage Monitoring system for small rodents. However, funding is currently unavailable, and she is considering taking a position as a communications specialist instead.
